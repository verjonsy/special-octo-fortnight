Here are some reasons why this webpage has good CSS:

  • The background color is light and easy on the eyes.
  • The font is easy to read and consistent throughout the page.
  • The header has a contrasting color to make it stand out.
  • The main content is centered and has a maximum width for readability.
  • The links are easy to distinguish from regular text.
  • The links change color when hovered over for visual feedback.
